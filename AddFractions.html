This app is intended as a tool to aid the teaching of adding fractions, where the crucial concept is lowest common denominator.

Fractions cannot be added when they have different denominators. They must first be expressed in such a way that they share a common denominator.

The user must follow this sequence of steps:

Choose the inputs (numerator and denominator for the fractions to be added).

Click 'Check input' (this includes checking that no improper fractions have been entered)

Click 'Add' to see the result.
